    Birthed from the River See Theatrical Jazz Performance Installation Written and Composed by Sharon Bridgforth - Featuring Sonja Parks The River See Story Bank is a virtual celebration of Divine Individuality and collective Offerings.

    The River See Theatrical Jazz Performance Installation explores blues stories as living arrangements of jazz. Through an improvisational process that happens live during performance/with singers, dancers and audience members, Bridgforth composes a moving soundscape in support of SEE’s Journey. Through this collective process all are responsible for this story/about Love. More about the performance HERE.

    The River See Story Bank is a virtual space for us to gather/to share our stories & connect. Come on JUMP IN won’t ya…

    You’re invited to submit a 2 minute (or less) response to the prompts (below).Offer: words, song, movement, silence, chants, visual images etc.Multi-lingual/sign language/gestural responses highly encouraged.If English is not your first language/your heart language PLEASE feel free to respond in whatever language your’s is! Submissions will be featured on the River See Story Bank.

    THE PROMPTS ARE: I Am . I Dr e a m . I Love . I Offer .

    There is no right or wrong. Be brave, be vulnerable.Respond from a place of passion and Joy.Let your story come through/in service of contributing to our collectiveRiver of beautiful Loving goodness. There is no deadline. Submissions will be accepted on an ongoing basis. You can submit your video via the Jump In button on this site.
